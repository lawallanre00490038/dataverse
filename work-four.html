Visa: Where you shop matters

2020 was a difficult year for many small businesses as the consequences of the pandemic forced them to close their doors. Visa aimed to bring Nigerian small businesses to the forefront and enable them to take their businesses online through the Visa Small Business Hub, a platform offering them tools and information on how to start, grow and manage their businesses digitally while also rallying consumers to make a big difference in their communities by shopping from these independent businesses.
